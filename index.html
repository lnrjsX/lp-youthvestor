Mission and vision

We aim to educate students across Indonesia on the value of investing, how to do so wisely, and to introduce them to what investing will be like in the future.

Our future plans

Every summer or winter vacation, we will conduct a boot camp to educate underprivileged children about the value of investing and how to get started.
